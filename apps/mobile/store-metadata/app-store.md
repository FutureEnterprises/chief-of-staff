*(4000 chars. Full marketing copy below.)*
```
You don't fail because you don't know what to do.
You fail in the exact same moments — over and over.

COYL catches them before they turn into a spiral.

Not a coach. Not a reminder app. Not a habit tracker.
An autopilot interruption system that fires surgical interrupts at the moments you usually lose — 9pm in the kitchen, the follow-up you promised, the pattern you keep repeating.

It's not the mistake. It's what you do after.

— WHAT COYL DOES —
1. Detect — Learns your danger windows, excuse patterns, and failure sequences. The shape of your autopilot before you see it.
2. Interrupt — Fires at the exact moment the script is about to run. Not scheduled reminders. Not motivation. Pattern recognition in your own voice.
3. Recover — Built for bad days, not perfect users. Slip, log it, move on. No Monday reset. Same-night re-entry.

— BUILT FIRST FOR WEIGHT LOSS —
• 9pm kitchen raids
• "I already messed up" spirals
• Weekend rebounds
• GLP-1 companion: catches the autopilot the drug doesn't

— WORKS ANYWHERE YOU KEEP SABOTAGING YOURSELF —
• Work follow-through — the email you didn't send, the action item you dropped
• Spending — the cart, the "I deserve this," the regret
• Focus — the tab switch, the tenth open of the same app
• Destructive patterns — the loop you keep returning to

— WHAT COYL SAYS IN THE MOMENT —
"You're not hungry. You're doing it again."
"You said you'd follow up. Did you?"
"Waiting is not action."
"You don't want the food. You want the feeling."

Reads steps, sleep, and weight from Apple Health to learn your stress patterns and fire interrupts when your body signals a danger window. Nothing is written back to Health. Permissions are entirely optional — COYL works fully without them.

— WHAT COYL IS NOT —

— PRIVACY —
Your data stays yours. No advertising trackers. No third-party analytics beyond what Expo and Apple require. Sensitive behavioral logs (slips, cravings, notes) are linked to your account only and never sold. Full policy: coyl.ai/privacy

Subscriptions available on coyl.ai. If you're already subscribed, sign in on mobile and your plan syncs automatically.

coyl.ai — stop the script before it runs your life.
```

## Privacy questionnaire (App Privacy tab)

Must match `apps/mobile/ios/COYL/PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy` exactly.

Data types collected and **linked to user**:

| Category | Types | Purpose | Used for tracking? |
|---|---|---|---|
| Contact Info | Email Address, Name | App Functionality, Analytics | No |
| Identifiers | User ID | App Functionality | No |
| Health & Fitness | Health (via HealthKit, optional), Fitness | App Functionality | No |
| Sensitive Info | Behavioral logs (slips, cravings, excuses) | App Functionality | No |
| User Content | Free-text notes typed when logging slips | App Functionality | No |
| Usage Data | Product Interaction | App Functionality, Analytics | No |

| Category | Types | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Diagnostics | Crash Data, Performance Data | App Functionality |
