# P1 — The GLP-1 Maintenance Gap

# 67% regain. 24 months. Pharma's billion-dollar churn.

## The convergent data

| Source | Cohort | Regain at 24 months |
|---|---|---|
| STEP-1 extension (Novo) | n=803, semaglutide 2.4mg | **~67%** of lost weight regained |
| SURMOUNT-4 follow-up (Lilly) | n=670, tirzepatide | **~63%** at 12 months off-drug |
| Lilly real-world evidence (May 2026 publication) | n=3,400 US patients | **~70%** at 24 months |
| Convergent estimate | All three cohorts | **~67% by Month 24** |

## What pharma loses per regained patient

- Loss of recurring prescription revenue: ~$1,200–$1,800/year per
  patient per drug
- Loss of payer-coverage continuity (payers move to outcomes-tiered
  reimbursement)
- Loss of category narrative ("Wegovy works" → "Wegovy works
  temporarily")

## The behavioral mechanism the drug doesn't touch

GLP-1s suppress appetite chemically. They do not touch:
- Late-evening eating (the 9–11 PM kitchen script)
- Weekend pattern (Friday-Saturday relaxation eating)
- Stress eating (cortisol spike correlation)
- Social eating (restaurant + family-meal anchoring)
- Reward-loop substitution (food as the only available regulation
  surface)

**These five patterns are 80% of the regain mechanism.** They are
exactly the surface area COYL's behavioral interrupt layer addresses.

MCP enables Claude to read your calendar. **PAP is what enables
Claude to propose that you reschedule, decide whether to fire the
proposal, fire it through the right modality, log the audit trail,
and feed the outcome back.** That's not a tool call. That's a
governance layer.

EAP is what enables Claude to fire the proposal as a haptic on
your Watch + a voice prompt on your iPhone + a screen-dim on your
MacBook, in a single coordinated cross-device action with atomic
all-or-none semantics. That's not a tool call either. That's a
device-fleet orchestration layer.
