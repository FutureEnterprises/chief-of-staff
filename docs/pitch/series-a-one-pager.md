## The opener

Every AI before COYL wraps around external reality — documents,
code, language. COYL is the first one wrapped around **behavioral
reality**. It reads HRV, accelerometer, screen-state, location,
and calendar — the passive signal stack a human emits without
typing — and fires inside the three-second window between
intention and behavior. The category jump is from smart journal
to thermostat. This is the substrate every foundation lab needs
to ship proactive AI, every pharma needs to solve GLP-1
maintenance, and every consumer needs to stop the script before
it runs their life.
