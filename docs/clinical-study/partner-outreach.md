## Outreach hygiene

- **Cap initial outreach at 3 sentences** before the protocol summary link.
  Anything longer reads as a pitch deck and gets archived.
- **Always lead with the partner's pain**, not COYL's product. Telehealth
  prescribers care about retention; labs care about publication; clinics care
  about patient outcomes.
- **Never claim outcomes we haven't measured.** The study is OPEN; no readout.
  Outreach claims must match `/clinical-study` exactly.
- **Never use the word "addiction"** in any partner email. Use "behavioral
  patterns" or "destructive loops." This is a regulatory and platform-safety
  posture; see `/recurring-loops` for the public-facing version of the
  same constraint.
- **Track every send in a single spreadsheet** with columns: org, contact,
  template, sent date, response date, outcome, notes. Not Salesforce.
  Spreadsheet, until volume justifies the upgrade.
- **Reply within 24h** to any inbound expression of interest. Speed of
  response is the single best predictor of conversion at this stage.
